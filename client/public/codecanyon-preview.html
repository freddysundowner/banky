Pick your favorite variant. Right-click any image and "Save as" or use the instructions below to capture at full resolution.

How to save as high-resolution image (590x300):

  1. Right-click on the variant you like
  2. Click Inspect Element to open DevTools
  3. Right-click the <div class="preview ..."> element in the Elements panel
  4. Select Capture node screenshot
  5. The image saves as a crisp 590x300 PNG
